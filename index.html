About Mahesh

Mahesh Babu is an Indian actor,producer,media personality,and philanthropist known for his works in Telugu cinema.he owns the production house G.Mahesh Babu Entertainment pvt.Ltd..The younger son of veteran telugu actor Krishna, Mahesh made his comeo as a child artist in Needa(1979),at the age of four,and acted in eight other films as a child artist.he made his debut as a lead actor with RajaKumarudu(1999) and won the state nandi award for male debut.Mahesh received breakthrough with the supernatural drama Murari(2001), and the action melodrama okkadu(2003). The former fetched him his first nandi Special jury Award and the latter fetched him his first Filmware Award for best actor-Telugu.The commercial successes of the action thriller's Pokiri and athadu establishes his national recognition and overseas records. He later went on to act in other commercially successes films such as dhukudu, Businessman, SVSC,Nenokkadine,sreemanthudu.to date he won seven Nandi awards,five Filmware awards,three south indian International Movie awards and one International Indian Film academy award.
